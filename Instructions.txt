The dataset consists of 4 related tables. PK = primary key, FK = foreign key

tStaff:
staffnumber (PK), firstname, lastname, programid (FK - tPrograms)

tProjects:
projectid (PK), projectcode, projectname, programid

tTrackingLog:
logid (PK), staffnumber (FK - tStaff), projectid (FK - tPrograms), hours, date

tPrograms:
programid (PK), programname, abbreviation
